April 28th, 1994

At April 22nd, McCormick, the creator/writer of Randy the Cannibal, was reported as missing after failure to appear at Midnight Channel's HQ and was unable to contact him. Later investigations revealed that his wallet and car was left at his home, and was last seen on Parsons Rd. in Madison, BN. If you have seen McCormick after April 22nd, or have additional information that can help the authorities locate him, please contact the Madison Police Department.
