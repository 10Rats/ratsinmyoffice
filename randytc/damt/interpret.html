Interpretations

There's a lot to unpack from that episode, so I'll keep it short and concise.


Diner: Might've appeared for the sake of familiarity, considering that this diner is near-identical to the one shown in a later episode.




Taxi: Although we never see the driver's face, some people recognized that his voice was similar to Cunningham's. This could be a plausible theory, as the driver can be interpreted as a guide to Randy's life.




Party: His dissatisfaction while at the party might represent anxiety, or maybe his feeling that he can't fit in with others?




Body in the bathroom: Oddly enough, the body isn't entirely dead as you can barely see its chest rising and sinking. Not entirely sure what it's supposed to symbolize. Might need a second opinion on this one.




Diner meal, champagne glass of blood, and the entirety of the final scene: Could be his cannibalistic urges manifesting in his dreams?
