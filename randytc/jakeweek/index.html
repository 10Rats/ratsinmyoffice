Thinking About DAMT...

March 30, 1994

It's pretty obvious that the entire episode was Randy's dream sequence. I can't help but try and come up with my own interpretation of his dream. Here it goes:

Diner: I'm not sure about the diner itself, but I feel like what happened there was supposed to be his urges manifesting in his dreams.

Taxi: I'm theorizing that the driver's Cunningham, since he's acting as a guide in Randy's life.

Party: His dissatisfaction of the party might represent anxiety, or maybe his feeling that he can't fit in with others?

Dead body: Not sure about this one, but that felt pretty significant.

Final scene: Another manifestation of his cannibalistic urges.

This is pretty much why I made this website. So if you're reading this, welcome.
