ARGs (Alternate Reality Games) generally spans across multiple mediums such as video games, websites, emails, and sometimes the outside world. They are often tied to a fictional narrative, but is not a requirement in order to be considered as an ARG. Sometimes it's made as part of an elaborate ad, or maybe it's just an art project.
