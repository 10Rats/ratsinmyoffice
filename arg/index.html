ARGs (Alternate Reality Games) are a form of interactive fiction that generally span across multiple mediums, usually with the goal of uncovering a mystery. Sometimes it's made as part of an elaborate ad, or it's just an art project.


If there's any ARG you want me to look into, send an email to contact[at]ratsinmyoffice[dot]org, and then I'll decide whether to investigate or not.
