Concepts

Transient Objects

First appeared in local Spacetime's 1970, objects originated from Deviation Omega (Shortened to "Deviation"). They're often mundane items such as coffee mugs or a book, but may contain information that doesn't line up with the in-universe timeline.


Spacetime Deviation

Aka alternate universes. Some are naturally occuring while a few are artificially made. Arthur Reese once suggested the use of wormholes to travel to different universes, but notes that anyone entering a wormhole will experience immense gravitational pull ("and my feet are a million miles away") and time dilation ("Have you ever watched a star die?").
